## Tips for Your Situation

1. **STEM OPT (Jun 29, 2026):** You can work C2C under STEM OPT as long as your employer is E-Verified
2. **H-1B Filing (May 2026):** Once H-1B petition is filed, you get Cap-Gap extension automatically
3. **C2C Structure:** You'll need to form your own LLC/Corp or work through an employer of record
4. **E-Verify Requirement:** For STEM OPT, the employer MUST be E-Verify enrolled
5. **Form I-983:** Required for STEM OPT - your C2C employer must complete this training plan
